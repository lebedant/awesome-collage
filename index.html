Steps to your awesome collage

1. Split horizontally

Right click on the canvas and select "Divide horizontally" in the context menu. The cutting line will split the canvas in the middle by default, but you can adjust it using a slider.

2. Split vertically

If you choose item "Divide vertically" in the context menu of the highlighted area, that area splits vertically in the middle (by default). As well as in the previous case, you can edit that split.

3. Change split value or delete nodes

In case if you change your mind, you always have possibility to change separation value or even remove some area. And if you divide area with assigned photo, the photo will be removed and the area will be split.

4. Add your photos

And finally, you can upload your favorite photos. Use the "plus" button to upload one or more files. Also, drag&drop is supported on the "photo tape". After that, just drag photos from the tape on the canvas.
